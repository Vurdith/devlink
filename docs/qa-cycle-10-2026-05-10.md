## Findings

### P1 - Post Detail Delete Menu Does Not Open Confirmation Dialog

- Route: `/p/[postId]`
- Account: throwaway local QA account
- Steps:
  - Create a throwaway post through the authenticated `/api/posts` path.
  - Open `/p/[postId]`.
  - Open the `Post actions` menu.
  - Click `Delete`.
- Expected: lazy `DeletePostDialog` opens with the `Delete Post` heading and Cancel/Delete actions.
- Actual: menu closes, no dialog appears, no console error is emitted, and the post remains visible.
- Likely area: `src/components/feed/PostDetail.tsx`, `src/components/feed/PostActionsMenu.tsx`, `src/components/feed/DeletePostDialog.tsx`.
